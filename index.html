Hi there! It's me, G. Welcome to my first ever personal site. Here you can find out a bit about me, see some of my photography, read my writing, and check out events that I have planned for my sorority!

I am a Junior at the University of Miami studying Print Journalism, with a minor in Religious Studies. Also, as a member of the Gamma Delta Chapter of Alpha Delta Pi, I served as the Director of Social Enrichment. Now, I am honored to announce that I have recently been elected as President of our chapter.

In addition, I work for the Cane Success Center on campus and I am the Greek Life Co-Editor for Miami's IBIS Yearbook.

The artistic genius' inspiring me right now are Halsey, r.m. drake, Lana Del Rey and rupi kaur.
