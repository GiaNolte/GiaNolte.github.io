As the Director of Social Enrichment for Alpha Delta Pi, I have had experience working with venues to plan and host events for crowds of 150 to 300 people. The planning process is extensive with responsibilities ranging from hiring off duty police officers, organizing food and catering, setting up decorations, brigning in DJs/photographers and organizing buses. The position also entails coordinating dates with fraternity social chairs and working within a $21,000 budget.

My passion for event planning truly stemmed from this honor in Alpha Delta Pi. I've always been drawn to weddings and love seeing the unique, elegance every wedding planner designs for the occasion. Soemday, I hope to use that inspiration and admiration to create my own wonderful events.
