I spoke in blossoming flowers
And gathered the dying tongues
Because lord knows they were still beautiful
Even in death
It takes a pure heart to see that the tragically damaged
Are withering stardust.
We must sprinkle a bit of them upon our souls
If we have any hope for saving true art.

“This journey will leave you breathless at times.
It will leave you speechless—
in awe of how delicate our hearts are and the shear beauty of life.
There will be times of heavy burden and sorrow,
But keep your eyes on the hopeful horizon.
Tomorrow is a new day and that is a remarkable beauty in itself.
Keep trekking for tomorrow, my friends.”
